[Astral's python-build-standalone](https://github.com/astral-sh/python-build-standalone),
release `20260510`.

The build project is distributed under MPL-2.0. CPython and bundled components retain their

such as pip. The launchers extract those files unchanged.

Exact upstream asset names and SHA-256 digests are recorded in
[`runtime/manifest.json`](runtime/manifest.json).
